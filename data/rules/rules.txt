Rules for the game :-

-> Press from 1 - 20 no. to choose between the boxes.
-> 0 for viewing answers (NOTE :- only when you have hearts)

Hearts :-

-> Easy Mode :- 3 hearts
-> Medium Mode :- 2 hearts
-> Hard Mode :- 1 heart

View Ans :-

-> Easy Mode :- Press any key to continue
-> Medium Mode :- after 10 seconds
-> Hard Mode :- after 5 seconds

Special Rule for (Hard Mode) :- boxes will reshuffle after 
every 5 wrong attempts.
